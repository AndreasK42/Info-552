I am a UI/UX Professional working on medical alerting devices in EHRs at the Information Science for Healthcare lab at Drexel University.

I am working in partnership with Children’s National Hospital and Rutgers University. The overall goal of our project is to develop a computer-aided approach for detecting PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) nonadherence in a group medical setting. I received a BFA in Communication Arts from Virginia Commonwealth University. I graduated during the height of the pandemic, but was able to find employment doing data visualization, graphic design, and illustration. Over time I learned about ui/ux, and started taking online classes through the Interactive Design Foundation. I fell in love with ui/ux, and enrolled at Drexel for their HCI/UX master’s program.
